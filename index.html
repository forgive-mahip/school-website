About school

"It is with this in mind that the Founding Members established MPS as a “model school” to bring quality education within the reach of working families and to prepare their children to engage with the global world as responsible and capable citizens.and being an “exemplar” school through progressive education principles and methodology of teaching and learning.Manjusri Public School is a coeducational school with two campuses. The City Campus catering to Grades LKG through IV is located in Gangtok, the state capital of Sikkim. The Country Campus catering to Grade V through XII is located at Temi Tarku, South Sikkim about two hours drive from Gangtok.Manjusri, the ""City School"" in the heart of Gangtok was an audacious plan back in 2009. We are proud to say that our dreams to establish an institution that imparts quality education came to fruition and now in 2016 we are into our seventh year!"
